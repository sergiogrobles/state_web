The Sunshine State

Florida, known as the "Sunshine State," is located in the southeastern region of the United States. It's bordered by the Gulf of Mexico to the west, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the states of Alabama and Georgia to the north. Florida has a diverse population with influences from various cultures, notably Latin American and Caribbean.
